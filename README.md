# AI-Assisted Community Catalogue

### AI-assisted. Community-governed. Fact-driven.

---

## An invitation

The AI-Assisted Community Catalogue is an open-source project to build a community-maintained catalogue of factual knowledge about products, services, organizations, places, events, and other entities.

Artificial Intelligence helps understand, extract, organize, and structure information.

People remain responsible for stewardship, governance, moderation, and continuous improvement.

This project exists for everyone.

---

## Vision

Enable anyone, anywhere, to contribute structured factual knowledge simply by describing or photographing something, while ensuring that information remains open, traceable, and continuously improvable.

---

## Mission

Build an open, AI-assisted catalogue that is:

- Community maintained
- Fact driven
- Transparent
- Traceable
- Respectful
- Extensible
- Free to use
- Open source

---

## Principles

We believe:

- Facts are more valuable than opinions.
- Transparency builds trust.
- AI assists people; it does not replace them.
- Communities create stronger knowledge than individuals working alone.
- Every contribution deserves respect.
- Every change should be traceable.
